About Us

A Story of Love & Community

Marie's Little Library was born in 2020 from the hearts of a mother and daughter who believed every child — no matter their circumstances — deserves access to books, art supplies, and music.

We serve children ages 2–18 in underserved communities, offering free book distributions, hands-on art workshops, and musical events that spark imagination and build confidence.

Every donation funds books, art kits, and scholarship opportunities for children who need it most. Together, we're writing a brighter story — one child at a time.
